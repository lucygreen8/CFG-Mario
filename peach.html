Princess Peach

Thanks for clicking to get to know Princess Peach!

Peach, (Japanese: ピーチ姫) is a major character in the Super Mario Bros universe, known for her gentle personality, kind temperement and affinity for the colour pink.

Peach's first appearance was in Super Mario Bros (1985) and was originally known for being the 'damsel in distress' and subject to Bowser's kidnappings and Mario's love interest.

However, in recent years, Peach has become more of a playable character with her own persionality. Go feminism!
